About Me: The Artist

Written by Kateleen De Guia



Kateleen De Guia is a student of AB Multimedia Arts in Lyceum of the Philippines University Manila. Greatly intrigued by her older brother's love for graphic design, she dreamed of becoming a graphic designer. Her love for traditional media stems from a birthday present, gifted a painting set from her bestfriend. Not knowing how to pick up a paintbrush, she listened to her passion and gave it a try. After her first portrait, more paintings have come along building this portfolio. Her love for painting continue to grew and she wishes to build her own physical exhibit one day.

Asides Traditional media, her creativity grows in digital media as well. Video editing and SFX. She started a freelance makeup artist journey last year and is looking forward to work with other clients.


Traditional Paintings

Described as her greatest passion and strength. She found happiness within colors and a great hobby to occupy her patience with mixing and building craft.

Sketches

She describes her sketches as a way for her mind and body to unwind. Astonished with the vintage style of early masters, her love for drawing studies grow.

Body Art and Tattooes

"My Body is like a temple and within these walls I tell a story." Her love for art manifests in her physical body. Adoration for tattooes and placements for things that matter to her.
